Problem

Music creation has gone mainstream, but recording software still thinks like a studio console.

Creators know the result they want, but most DAWs ask them to think in buses, inserts, sends, routing, plugin chains, compression, EQ and mastering menus.

Solution

One human-language surface for making finished music.

PHATSO gives creators the band, the pocket, the sound and the finish on one surface.
